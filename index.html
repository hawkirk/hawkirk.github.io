Hello! My name is harrison kirk.

I aspire to use data and technology to help policymakers identify solutions to inequity in education and public health.

R and Python are my preferred tools for analysis, modeling, and visualization. I’m also interested in backend technologies like cloud computing and APIs. For fun, I like playing around with web dev tools, Linux, design software, and experimenting with anything new.

Check out my resume for more on my experience and skills.

In my free time I love exploring nature, creative writing, traveling, producing experimental electronic music, and many other things.
